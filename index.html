Behind the Lens

I'm a photographer, videographer, livestreamer, photo-editor, video-editor with over two years of experience, developed through my work at Agahozo Shalom Youth Village and by capturing weddings and a variety of events. I have skills in camera operation, lighting setup, live streaming production, video and photo post-production, storytelling, client communication, and time management.

My approach focuses on documenting authentic moments and telling stories through natural, meaningful images where every frame is a collaboration between light, emotion and timing.

Leading Through the Lens

As a media leader, I have directed visual storytelling projects that amplify voices and preserve moments of significance. From coordinating photo coverage at major events to mentoring emerging photographers, media leadership is about vision and execution.

I believe that powerful imagery can shift perspectives, and leading a media team means ensuring every frame carries intention and impact.

Serving the Community

Society leadership is rooted in service. Through my involvement at Agahozo Shalom Youth Village and beyond, I have learned that true leadership means listening first, empowering others, and creating spaces where everyone feels seen and valued.

Whether organizing community initiatives or simply being present for someone who needs support, I lead with empathy and a deep commitment to uplifting those around me.
